S5.2-A — OFFICE DASHBOARD SCOPE FREEZE
=====================================

ROLE
----
Office User (Operations)

PRIMARY GOAL
------------
Run daily operations safely without altering historical data.

ALLOWED CAPABILITIES
--------------------
1) Live Delivery Monitoring
   - View active delivery men & trips
   - Vehicle-wise status (en-route / completed)
   - Area-wise customer lists (read-only)

2) Cash Operations (CONTROLLED WRITE)
   - Opening cash entry
   - Cash in / cash out
   - Denomination entry
   - Closing cash
   - NO back-dated edits

3) CSV Operations
   - Morning BPCL CSV upload (new day only)
   - De-duplication as per S4 rules
   - No overwrite of delivered customers

4) Manual Corrections (LOGGED)
   - Mark customer not available
   - Reschedule delivery
   - Emergency delivery tagging
   - Every correction must create an audit log

READ-ONLY SECTIONS
------------------
- Stock ledger (view only)
- Wages (view only)
- BPCL comparison (view only)
- Owner summaries (view only)

FORBIDDEN ACTIONS
-----------------
- No schema changes
- No deletion of records
- No modification of historical days
- No wage recalculation
- No stock back-adjustment

UI PRINCIPLES
-------------
- Desktop-first
- Fast keyboard-driven workflows
- English + Marathi mandatory
- Status colors as per S4

ANCHORS (IMMUTABLE)
-------------------
- Uses existing S3/S4/S5 APIs
- Does NOT duplicate logic
- All writes must go through existing services

FREEZE STATUS
-------------
This scope is LOCKED.
Any change requires update to ERP_FREEZE.md.
