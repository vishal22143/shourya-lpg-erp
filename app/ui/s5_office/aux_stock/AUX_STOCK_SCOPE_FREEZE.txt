S5.2-E — OFFICE AUXILIARY STOCK (SCOPE FREEZE)
=============================================

MODULE PURPOSE
--------------
Track office-controlled auxiliary items and cylinder counts
without affecting core LPG stock, delivery, wages, or BPCL logic.

ITEMS COVERED
-------------
A) Cylinder Counts (COUNT ONLY)
   - 14.2 KG Filled
   - 14.2 KG Empty
   - 5 KG Filled
   - 5 KG Empty

B) Accessories
   - Blue Book
   - Rubber Hose / Suraksha Pipe

TIME BOUNDARY
-------------
- Month-based accounting only
- Daily operations derive from monthly opening

OPENING STOCK (CRITICAL RULE)
-----------------------------
- Opening stock is entered ONCE per month (morning of Day 1)
- After save:
    * NO edit
    * NO delete
    * NO overwrite
- Any correction requires:
    * Manager approval
    * Separate adjustment record
    * Audit log

MOVEMENT & SALES LOGIC
----------------------
- No manual math
- Sales auto-accumulate during the month
- Movements only via defined actions:
    * Sale
    * Issue
    * Return (if applicable)
- All movements are append-only

PHYSICAL STOCK (MONTH END)
--------------------------
- Physical stock captured once at month end
- System computes:
    Opening
  + Receipts
  - Sales
  = Expected Stock

VARIANCE & ACCOUNTABILITY
-------------------------
- Variance = Physical - Expected
- Variance highlighted automatically
- Variance value assigned to:
    * Office staff
    * Manager advance
- No silent adjustment allowed

UI & ACCESS RULES
-----------------
- Office dashboard ONLY
- Owner / Accountant: read-only
- Desktop-first
- English + Marathi mandatory

NON-IMPACT GUARANTEE
--------------------
- Does NOT affect:
    * LPG stock ledger
    * Delivery logic
    * Wages
    * BPCL reconciliation
    * Owner day-end totals

FREEZE STATUS
-------------
This scope is LOCKED.
Implementation must follow this exactly.
